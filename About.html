About Us

The University of Rizal System (URS) as an organized academic entity composed of separate but interrelated units coordinates and integrates system-wide functions and activities. It has the prime mandate of providing instruction, research, extension and production to the public.
With the Board of Regents as the highest governing board, the University is also led by its president, Dr. Nancy Talavera Pascual. With her, are three vice-presidents - Dr. Renato F. De Lemon, Vice-president for Administration and Finance; Dr. Allan E. Conde, Vice-president for Academic Affairs; and, Dr. Marites M. Rio, Vice-president for Research Development, Extension and Production. The University is also managed by campus directors, unit heads, deans and program heads.
The President is the chief executive officer, university leader and spokesperson. She performs her administrative and leadership work and participates in related activities such as instruction, extension, production and research overseeing all university operations, including programs and policy directions.

About COS

The College of Science is a division within a university that focuses on scientific disciplines such as physics, chemistry, biology, mathematics, and computer science. It is typically one of the largest and most important colleges within a university, as science and technology are critical to many fields and industries. The College of Science usually offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate degree programs, ranging from bachelor's degrees to doctoral degrees. These programs are designed to provide students with a strong foundation in their chosen scientific field, as well as the skills and knowledge necessary to pursue careers in research, academia, industry, or government. In addition to academic coursework, the College of Science often provides opportunities for students to participate in research projects, internships, and other hands-on learning experiences. This allows students to apply their knowledge in real-world settings and gain practical skills that are highly valued by employers. Overall, the College of Science plays a crucial role in advancing scientific knowledge and preparing students for successful careers in the sciences.
